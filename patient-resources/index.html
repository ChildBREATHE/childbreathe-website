Patient Resources

General information for patients and families about the ChildBREATHE research project.

About this section

This section provides general information for patients, parents, and families with an interest in the ChildBREATHE project.

The website is primarily intended for collaborators, researchers, and professional stakeholders. However, we recognise the importance of making key project information accessible to patients and families as well.

Clinical context

ChildBREATHE is closely linked to established clinical settings at specialised paediatric centres in Denmark.

Participation and project-related procedures take place within ordinary clinical care pathways and in accordance with relevant ethical and regulatory frameworks.

What the project aims to improve

The overall aim of the project is to improve the understanding of bronchiectasis in children and adolescents in Denmark.

This includes research on disease mechanisms, microbiology, inflammation, clinical trajectories, and long-term outcomes. A better understanding of these factors may support improved diagnostics, follow-up, and future treatment strategies.

For families seeking further information

Families who would like further practical information about the project are encouraged to contact their local clinical team or the study contact listed on the website.
