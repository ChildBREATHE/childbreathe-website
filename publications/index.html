Scope of publications

ChildBREATHE publications address clinical, microbiological, and immunological aspects of paediatric bronchiectasis, with a particular focus on disease mechanisms, airway inflammation, microbiota, and longitudinal clinical outcomes.

Future outputs from the programme will include work related to registry-based research, translational analyses, and national multicentre data on children and adolescents with bronchiectasis in Denmark.
